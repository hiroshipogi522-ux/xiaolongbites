Business Introduction

Xiao Long Bites is a dessert business in Marilao, Bulacan that introduces a new way of enjoying xiao long bao by offering it in sweet flavors. The business starts by selling its first three flavors: ube, chocolate, and mango, all served in eco-friendly packaging. The owners aim to create a unique dessert experience that combines familiarity with innovation while promoting sustainability.

Xiao Long Bites is envisioned to grow beyond its starting point by first establishing a strong presence in the local community, particularly through school events and social media promotion. After gaining success in its initial location, the business will gradually expand to other municipalities and cities. Along with expansion, the menu will also grow to include more dessert variations based on customer feedback and taste testing. With proper promotion and development, Xiao Long Bites seeks to reach larger markets and become the leading dessert xiao long bao brand for food lovers and sweet enthusiasts.

Product Background & Flavors

Our first three delightful flavors are inspired by local culture and ingredients:

  • Ube
  • Chocolate
  • Mango (using San Rafael mangoes)

The mango flavor is made from the famous San Rafael mangoes, known for their sweetness and rich flavor. This connection to San Rafael culture highlights our commitment to celebrating local produce and traditions while offering a fresh twist on the classic xiao long bao dessert.

Cultural Background

Xiao long bao, traditionally a savory Chinese steamed dumpling, is reimagined by Xiao Long Bites as a sweet dessert that resonates with the rich culinary heritage of San Rafael, Bulacan. San Rafael is known for its bountiful mango orchards and vibrant local delicacies, which inspire our unique mango flavor using authentic San Rafael mangoes.

The use of ube, a beloved purple yam native to the Philippines, connects our product to Filipino dessert traditions, while the chocolate flavor adds a modern twist that appeals to a wide range of tastes. By blending these flavors with the familiar form of xiao long bao, Xiao Long Bites bridges cultural influences and local pride, creating a dessert that honors San Rafael’s agricultural roots and its community’s love for innovative sweets.

Moreover, Xiao Long Bites preserves and celebrates Filipino cultural identity by incorporating indigenous flavors like ube and San Rafael mangoes into a globally recognized delicacy. This fusion not only showcases the richness of Filipino ingredients but also promotes pride in local heritage, making every bite a delightful reminder of the Philippines’ unique culinary story.
