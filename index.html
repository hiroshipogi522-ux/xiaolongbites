Flavor Background

Our dessert dumplings come in three delightful flavors. The mango flavor is made using the famous San Rafael mangoes, known for their exceptional sweetness and quality.

Ube

A beloved Filipino purple yam flavor.

Chocolate

Rich and creamy for chocolate lovers.

Mango

Made with authentic San Rafael mangoes.

Cultural Background

The use of San Rafael mangoes connects our product deeply to the local culture and agricultural heritage of San Rafael, Bulacan. This region is celebrated for its vibrant food culture and quality produce. By incorporating these mangoes, Xiao Long Bites honors local traditions while offering a fresh, innovative dessert experience.

Our eco-friendly packaging also reflects our commitment to sustainability and respect for the environment, values that resonate strongly within our community.
