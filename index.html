Business Introduction

Xiao Long Bites is a dessert business in Marilao, Bulacan that introduces a new way of enjoying xiao long bao by offering it in sweet flavors. The business would start off by selling its first three flavors: ube, chocolate, and mango, all served in eco-friendly packaging. The owners aim to create a unique dessert experience that combines familiarity with innovation while promoting sustainability.

Xiao Long Bites is envisioned to grow beyond its starting point by first establishing a strong presence in the local community, particularly through school events and social media promotion. After gaining success in its initial location, the business would gradually expand to other municipalities and cities. Along with expansion, the menu would also grow to include more dessert variations based on customer feedback and taste testing. With proper promotion and development, Xiao Long Bites seeks to reach larger markets and become the leading dessert xiao long bao brand for food lovers and sweet enthusiasts.

Flavor Background

Our dessert dumplings come in three delightful flavors: Ube, Chocolate, and Mango. The mango flavor is made using the famous San Rafael mangoes, known for their exceptional sweetness and quality.
